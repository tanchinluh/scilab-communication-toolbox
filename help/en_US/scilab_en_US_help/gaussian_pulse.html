gaussian_pulse

Gaussian pulse

Calling Sequence

y = gaussian_pulse(t,BT)

Parameters

t:

time, in multiple of symbol period (1 = 1 symbol period)

BT:

Bandwidth-time product

Description

Compute a gaussian filter with given Bandwidth Time product.

Examples

t = linspace(-10,10,1000);
y = gaussian_pulse(t,0.5);
plot(t,y);
