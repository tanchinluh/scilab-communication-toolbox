comm_tbx >> Modulation / demodulation

Modulation / demodulation

  • demod_init — Initialization of a demodulator object.
  • demod_process — Complete demodulation chain implementation (from a RF or IF signal to a binary sequence).
  • mod_init — Creation of a modulator object
  • mod_process — Modulation (binary vector to RF or IF output)
  • wf_init — Initialization of a waveform object
  • wf_set_filter — Specification of the pulse shaping filter for a waveform
