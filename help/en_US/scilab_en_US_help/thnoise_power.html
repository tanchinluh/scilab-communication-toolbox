thnoise_power

Compute thermal noise power

Calling Sequence

[N,Ndb] = thnoise_power(bandwidth, T)
[N,Ndb] = thnoise_power(bandwidth)

Parameters

bw:

Noise bandwidth, in Hz

T:

Ambiant temperature, in ° Celcius (default is 25 °C)

N:

Noise power, in W

Ndb:

Noise power, in dBm (0 dBm = 1 mW)
