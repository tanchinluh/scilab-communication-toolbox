gaussian_fir

FIR approximation of gaussian filter

Parameters

BT:

Bandwidth-time product

osf:

over sampling factor

n:

number of coefficients of the FIR filter

Calling Sequence

y = gaussian_fir(r,osf,n)

Description

Examples

h = gaussian_fir(BT=0.8,osf=5,n=21);
clf(); plot_rimp(h);
