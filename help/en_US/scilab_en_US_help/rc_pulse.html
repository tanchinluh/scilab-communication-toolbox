rc_pulse

Raised cosine pulse

Calling Sequence

y = rc_pulse(t,roll_off)

Parameters

t:

time, in multiple of symbol period (1 = 1 symbol period)

r:

roll-off factor

Description

Compute

Examples

t = linspace(-10,10,1000);
y = rc_pulse(t,0.5);
