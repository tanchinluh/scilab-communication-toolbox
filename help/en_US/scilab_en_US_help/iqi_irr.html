iqi_irr

Compute Image Rejection Ratio (IRR), given the gain and phase imbalance of a quadrature mixer

Calling Sequence

IRR = iqi_irr(g,phi);

Parameters

g:

Relative gain imbalance

phi:

Phase imbalance (in radians)

IRR:

Image Rejection Ratio (power ratio)

Description

Result is given in power ratio (use 10*log10(IRR) to get dB units).
